About CheckMyDeposit

CheckMyDeposit is a trading name of FairPlane UK Limited. We are a firm of solicitors dealing exclusively with consumer rights, including tenancy deposit claims.

Our service

CheckMyDeposit assists tenants in making compensation claims against landlords who have failed to protect their deposits according to the law.

All tenants who rent flats or houses and have paid a rental deposit are protected under the Government Tenancy Deposit scheme. This means that landlords are required to register rental deposits paid to them with one of three Government tenancy deposit schemes. The landlord is required to provide the tenant with a certificate confirming that the deposit was registered correctly.

Failure to register a deposit and/or provide the required information to the tenant within 30 days of the deposit being made, entitles the tenant to receive compensation between one and three times the deposit sum originally paid.

Claims can be made up to 6 years after the date the deposit was paid, as the rule applies to both current and old tenancies.

Even if a tenant received all of their deposit back at the end of their tenancy, they are still entitled to make a claim for compensation if their deposit wasn’t correctly protected.
